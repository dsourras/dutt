Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how dutt may collect, use, and protect personal information when users interact with the website, contact forms, and related communication channels.

1. Information we may collect

We may collect information that users voluntarily submit through forms, such as name, phone number, email address, city, vehicle type, availability, and any message the user chooses to provide.

We may also receive technical information that is automatically generated by browsers or third-party tools, such as basic device, browser, and usage data.

2. How we use the information

Information may be used to respond to inquiries, review driver applications, communicate with users, improve services, and maintain website functionality and security.

We do not use submitted information for purposes unrelated to the operation, communication, or improvement of the dutt platform.

3. Communications

If a user submits a contact form or application form, dutt may contact that user using the details provided in order to respond, continue communication, or evaluate a possible collaboration.

4. Third-party services

The website may use third-party services or infrastructure providers, such as hosting, form submission tools, analytics tools, or app store links. These services may process certain information according to their own privacy terms.

Users should also review the privacy policies of any third-party services they interact with through the website.

5. Data security

Reasonable efforts may be taken to protect submitted information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. However, no online system can guarantee absolute security.

6. Data retention

Information may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to manage communication, evaluate applications, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or support legitimate business operations.

7. User rights

Users may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information they have submitted, subject to applicable legal and operational requirements.

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updated version published on this page will replace the previous version.
